About Shawn

Shawn currently has a job in Nashville, TN, maintaining a legacy jumble of often poorly-coded JS, Node, and React. Shawn likes to pretend this experience is a bit like exploring an alien planet, attempting to make sense of the truly bizarre and nonsensical geography, ocassionally discovering relics from past explorers, but mostly stumbling around with only a general sense of direction and minimal tools to progress.

This is not at all accurate. But Shawn likes to pretend it all the same.
